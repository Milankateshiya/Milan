what is a different tag to separate section of text?

separate a section of texts in HTML using the tags:

<br> tag It is used to separate the line of text. It breaks the current line and shifts the flow of the text to a new line.

<p> tag This tag is used to write a paragraph of text.

Section tag is used to distribute the content

the section of documents such as chapters, headers, footers, or any other section

<section> Section Contents </section>
